Sample Epistemology Questions for You

1. How do you know that what you believe is true?

2. Can we ever have absolute certainty, or is all knowledge provisional?

3. How do you decide which sources to trust?

4. Are your perceptions of the world accurate, or filtered by bias?

5. What is the difference between belief, opinion, and knowledge?

6. Can two people “know” the same thing differently?

7. Is ignorance always bad, or can it sometimes be useful?
